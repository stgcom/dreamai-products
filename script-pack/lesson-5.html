📖 In This Lesson

Phone scripts are great — but sometimes you need it in writing. These 8 email templates are the written versions of your highest-performing call scripts, adapted for email with subject lines, proper formatting, and CTAs.

✉️
Email Templates 8 Templates
1
Welcome Email (New Lead)
Send immediately after first inquiry
Subject: Welcome to [BusinessName], [FirstName]!Hi [FirstName], Thanks for reaching out to [BusinessName]! I'm [AIName], your dedicated point of contact here. I'd love to learn more about what you're looking for. In the meantime, here's what you can expect from us: • A response within [responseTime] hours • A no-pressure consultation if you'd like to learn more • Answers to any questions you have — no question is too small Would you have 10 minutes this week for a quick call? You can book directly here: [BookingLink] Looking forward to connecting, [AIName] [BusinessName]
Customize: Set [responseTime] to your actual SLA. Add 2-3 social proof bullet points or a case study link after the signature.
2
Follow-Up Email (No Response)
Send 48 hours after no reply to initial outreach
Subject: Quick follow-up — [BusinessName]Hi [FirstName], I reached out a couple of days ago and just wanted to follow up. I know things get busy! If now isn't the right time, no worries at all — just let me know, and I'll check back in [timeframe] without being pushy. If you're interested but have questions, I'm happy to hop on a 10-minute call at your convenience: [BookingLink] Either way, I'm here when you need me. Best, [AIName] [BusinessName]
Customize: Set [timeframe] (e.g., "a few weeks"). Keep the tone light — never guilt-trip. This works best when the first email was value-first, not sales-heavy.
3
Appointment Confirmation Email
Send immediately after booking
Subject: Your appointment is confirmed — [Date] at [Time]Hi [FirstName], Great news! Your appointment is confirmed. Here are the details: 📅 Date: [DayOfWeek], [Date] 🕐 Time: [Time] 📍 Location: [Address or Video Link] 👤 Service: [ServiceType] What to bring: • [Required document 1] • [Required document 2] Please arrive 5 minutes early. If you need to reschedule, just reply to this email or call us at [PhoneNumber]. We're looking forward to seeing you! Best, [BusinessName]
Customize: Add Google Calendar / iCal links. For telehealth: replace "Location" with video link + "Test your camera 5 minutes early."
4
Appointment Reminder Email
Send 24 hours before appointment
Subject: Reminder: Your [ServiceType] appointment tomorrowHi [FirstName], Just a friendly reminder about your appointment tomorrow: 📅 [DayOfWeek], [Date] at [Time] 📍 [Address or Video Link] Quick checklist: ☐ [Prep item 1] ☐ [Prep item 2] If anything has changed or you need to reschedule, just reply to this email or call [PhoneNumber]. See you tomorrow! [BusinessName]
Customize: The checklist is key — it reduces no-shows by giving them specific prep tasks. Add "Reply YES to confirm" as the first line for higher engagement.
5
Post-Visit Follow-Up Email
Send 24–48 hours after appointment
Subject: How was your visit, [FirstName]?Hi [FirstName], Thank you for visiting [BusinessName] on [VisitDate]! We hope everything went well. A few things you might need: • [Follow-up resource or link] • [Next steps or action items] If you have any questions or concerns about your visit, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here for you. If you had a great experience, we'd really appreciate a quick review on [Platform]. It helps others find us: [ReviewLink] Thanks again, [BusinessName]
Customize: Only include the review ask if the visit went well. For medical: add "If you're experiencing any issues, please call us immediately."
6
Price/Proposal Email
Send after a qualified inquiry for high-ticket services
Subject: Your custom proposal from [BusinessName]Hi [FirstName], Thanks for the great conversation! Based on what you shared about [their needs], I've put together a proposal tailored to your situation: 📋 **Scope:** [ServiceDescription] 💰 **Investment:** [Price] ⏰ **Timeline:** [Timeline] 🎁 **What's included:** • [Deliverable 1] • [Deliverable 2] • [Deliverable 3] I know this is a meaningful investment. Most of our clients see a return within [ROI timeframe]. Want to talk it through? Book a quick call: [BookingLink] Looking forward to working with you, [AIName]
Customize: Always personalize the "Scope" line to reference their specific needs. Include a testimonial or case study from a similar client. Add a soft deadline: "This proposal is valid for 7 days."
7
Referral Request Email
Send to happy customers after positive feedback
Subject: [FirstName], we have a favor to ask 🙏Hi [FirstName], I'm so glad you had a great experience with [BusinessName]! Your satisfaction means the world to us. We grow primarily through word of mouth, and we'd love your help. If you know anyone who could benefit from our [ServiceType], we'd be incredibly grateful for the introduction. As a thank-you, we offer [ReferralIncentive] for every successful referral. Here's a quick message you can share: _"I've been working with [BusinessName] and had a great experience. If you need [ServiceType], I'd recommend reaching out — here's their info: [WebsiteLink]"_ Just forward this email to anyone who comes to mind, or reply with their contact info and we'll take it from there. Thank you! [AIName]
Customize: The pre-written message makes it effortless for them. Include a one-click referral link if you have one. Only send to genuinely satisfied customers.
8
Re-Engagement Email
Send to leads who went cold after 30+ days
Subject: Still thinking about [ServiceType], [FirstName]?Hi [FirstName], It's been a while since we last connected, and I wanted to check in. A lot has changed since then: 🆕 [New update or feature 1] 🆕 [New update or feature 2] 📈 [Social proof — "We've now helped X clients"] I'm not here to pressure you — just want to make sure you have the latest info in case the timing is right now. If you're still considering [ServiceType], here's a quick link to book a 10-minute call: [BookingLink] And if your needs have changed, no problem — just let me know and I'll update your preferences. Best, [AIName] [BusinessName]
Customize: The "new updates" section gives them a reason to re-engage. Add a limited-time offer or bonus if you have one. If no reply, move them to a quarterly nurture sequence.
