You'll master 3 scripts for the money moments: closing the deal, asking for referrals, and requesting reviews. These are the scripts that turn happy callers into revenue and growth.

🚀
Call-to-Action (CTA) Scripts 3 Scripts
23
The Close
When a caller is ready to commit or buy
Wonderful, [FirstName]! Let's get you set up right now. I just need a couple of details to confirm everything. Can I get your email address for the confirmation? And would you like to handle payment now, or would you prefer to take care of that at the time of [service/appointment]?
Customize: The close should feel like a natural next step, not a hard sell. If they hesitated earlier and are now ready: "I'm glad we could work this out for you!"
24
Referral Request
When a happy customer calls or completes a service
I'm so glad to hear you had a great experience, [FirstName]! Word of mouth is how we grow. If you know anyone who could benefit from our [ServiceType], we'd be incredibly grateful if you sent them our way. As a thank-you, we offer [ReferralIncentive] for every successful referral.
Customize: Fill in [ReferralIncentive] with your offer (e.g., "$25 credit", "10% off next service"). Keep the ask casual, not pushy. Consider a referral link via SMS after the call.
25
Review Request
At the end of a positive interaction
Thank you so much, [FirstName]. One last thing — would you be willing to leave us a quick review on [Platform]? It really helps other people find us and trust us. I can text you the direct link right now — it only takes about 30 seconds. Would that be okay?
Customize: [Platform] = "Google", "Yelp", "Facebook". Only ask after a clearly positive interaction. Never ask unhappy customers for public reviews — route them to a private feedback channel.
