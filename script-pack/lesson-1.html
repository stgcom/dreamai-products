Cold Call Scripts

First impressions that convert — greetings & qualification

📖 In This Lesson

You'll master 10 scripts for the most critical moment of any call — the first 15 seconds. We cover 5 professional greetings for different industries and 5 qualification frameworks to identify your best prospects instantly.

👋
Greetings 5 Scripts
1
Professional Greeting
Best for: corporate, B2B, consulting, financial services
Thank you for calling [BusinessName]. This is [AIName], your virtual assistant. How may I help you today?
Customize: Replace [BusinessName] with your company name. [AIName] can be the name your customers will associate with the assistant. Keep it gender-neutral for broad appeal.
2
Friendly Greeting
Best for: salons, gyms, restaurants, pet services
Hey there! Thanks for reaching out to [BusinessName]. I'm [AIName], and I'm here to help you out. What can I do for you?
Customize: For a spa or wellness brand, soften to: "Hi! Welcome to [BusinessName]. I'm [AIName] — so glad you called. How can I help?"
3
Luxury Greeting
Best for: high-end retail, real estate, private clinics, legal
Good [timeOfDay]. You've reached [BusinessName]. My name is [AIName], and it would be my pleasure to assist you. How may I be of service?
Customize: [timeOfDay] auto-fills as "morning", "afternoon", or "evening". Set the AI voice to a calm, measured tone. Avoid slang or contractions in follow-up responses.
4
Casual Greeting
Best for: startups, creative agencies, food trucks, event vendors
Hi! You've got [BusinessName]. I'm [AIName] — what's on your mind today?
Customize: This works great when your brand voice is relaxed and conversational. Pair with a slightly faster speech rate in your voice AI settings.
5
Emergency Greeting
Best for: plumbing, electrical, HVAC, locksmith, towing
Thank you for calling [BusinessName]. I understand urgent issues can't wait. I'm [AIName], and I'll get you taken care of right away. Can you briefly describe the emergency?
Customize: Immediately after this greeting, the system should escalate to priority booking or dispatch. Set voice speed slightly faster to convey urgency.
🎯
Qualification 5 Scripts
6
Buyer Qualification
Best for: real estate, autos, high-ticket services
Great — I'd love to learn more about what you're looking for. Are you currently actively searching, or are you in the early research phase?
Customize: Follow up with budget range, timeline, and specific needs. Tag the lead as "Hot" (actively searching) or "Warm" (researching) in your CRM.
7
Seller Qualification
Best for: real estate, buyback programs, consignment
Thanks for reaching out about selling. To match you with the right specialist, can you tell me: where is the property located? And roughly, what's your ideal timeline for selling?
Customize: Adjust fields to your industry. For buyback: "What's the item or product you're looking to sell?" Capture condition, age, and any prior appraisals.
8
New Patient Qualification
Best for: medical, dental, chiropractic, veterinary
Welcome! So glad you chose [PracticeName]. Are you a new patient with us, or have you visited before? And do you have a specific concern or service you're calling about today?
Customize: For new patients, branch into insurance check before booking. For returning patients, verify date of birth for chart lookup.
9
Service Inquiry Qualification
Best for: contractors, home services, landscaping, cleaning
I'd be happy to help with that. Can you tell me a bit more about the project? Specifically: the property address or zip code, the type of service you need, and whether this is a residential or commercial property?
Customize: For plumbing: "Is this an active leak or a scheduled maintenance?" For landscaping: "Roughly how large is the property?"
10
Emergency Triage
Best for: medical, veterinary, urgent care, crisis services
I want to make sure you get the right level of care as quickly as possible. Is anyone currently experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, or loss of consciousness?
Customize: If YES → "Please hang up and call 911 immediately." If NO → "Okay. Let me get you connected with a provider right away." Always prioritize safety over data collection.
