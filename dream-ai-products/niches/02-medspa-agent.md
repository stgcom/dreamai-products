## Agent Capabilities

### 1. AI Booking & Scheduling Agent
**Handles:** Appointment requests (call, SMS, website chat)

**Flows:**
- New patient → intake questions → book consultation
- Existing patient → quick booking → confirm history
- Cancellation → fill from waitlist automatically
- Reschedule → find alternative slots

**Qualification (new patients):**
1. What treatment are you interested in?
2. Have you had this treatment before?
3. Are you a new or returning patient?
4. Any contraindications? (medical history filter)
5. Preferred date/time?

### 2. Pre/Post-Care Support Agent
**Pre-care:** Sends instructions 48h and 24h before appointment
- "Please avoid sun exposure for 48 hours before your treatment"
- "Arrive with clean skin, no makeup"
- "Eat a light meal 2 hours before"

**Post-care:** Follow-up at 2h, 24h, 72h after treatment
- "How are you feeling after your treatment today?"
- "Remember to apply SPF 30+ for the next 7 days"
- "If you experience any unusual redness or swelling, call us immediately"

**FAQ handling:**
- Pricing for treatments
- Recovery timelines
- Contraindications
- Financing options

### 3. No-Show Prevention System
**Layer 1:** Booking confirmation (instant)
**Layer 2:** 48h reminder (SMS + option to reschedule)
**Layer 3:** 2h day-of reminder
**Layer 4:** If no-show → automated follow-up + rebooking offer
**Layer 5:** Deposit requirement for repeat no-shows (configurable)

**Result:** Reduces no-show rate from 20–30% to 5–10%

### 4. Lead Qualification & Consultation Booking
**Triggers:** Website inquiry, social media DM, phone call
**Qualifies:** Budget, treatment interest, timeline, medical history
**Books:** Free consultation with provider
**Escalates:** Complex medical questions → human staff

### 5. Review Solicitation Agent
**Trigger:** 24h after appointment completion
**Flow:**
- "We hope you loved your experience at [MedSpa]! Would you mind leaving us a quick review?"
- [Direct link to Google/Yelp]
- If 4–5 stars → "Thank you! Would you share that on Google?"
- If 1–3 stars → "We're sorry to hear that. Let us make it right." → Alert owner
