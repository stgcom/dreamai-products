## Agent Capabilities

### 1. AI Lead Receptionist (Voice + SMS)
**Handles:** Inbound calls, website chat, SMS inquiries

**Scripts:**
- "Hi, thanks for calling [Agency]! I'm Sarah, your AI assistant. Are you looking to buy, sell, or just exploring?"
- Qualifies: timeline, budget range, location preference, pre-approval status
- Books qualified leads directly into agent's calendar
- Transfers to human for complex/sensitive calls

**Qualification Flow:**
1. Buying or selling?
2. Timeline? (urgent / 1–3 months / just browsing)
3. Location preference?
4. Budget range or price range for selling?
5. Pre-approved? (buyers) / Have a home to sell? (sellers)
6. Best time for a showing/consultation?

### 2. Speed-to-Lead Auto-Responder
**Triggers:** New web lead, Zillow/Realtor.com inquiry, Facebook lead ad
**Actions:**
- Instant SMS within 60 seconds
- If no response in 5 min → voice call
- Escalates to human if lead is hot (ready to transact in <30 days)

**SMS Template:**
"Hi [Name], this is [Agent Name]'s assistant. I saw you were looking at homes in [Area]. I have a few properties that might be perfect — want me to send some over, or better yet, schedule a quick call?"

### 3. CRM Auto-Fill Agent
**Monitors:** Call recordings, email threads, SMS conversations
**Extracts:** Name, phone, email, property interest, budget, timeline, notes
**Populates:** CRM fields (works with Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, LionDesk, HubSpot)

### 4. Listing Description Writer
**Input:** Property details (beds, baths, sqft, features, neighborhood)
**Output:** MLS-compliant description in multiple styles:
- Professional/formal
- Warm/storytelling
- Luxury/high-end
- Investment-focused

### 5. Post-Close Nurture Agent
**Triggers:** 30/60/90/180/365 days after closing
**Messages:**
- "Just checking in — how's the new home? Need any contractor recommendations?"
- Seasonal: "Winter is coming — here's a home maintenance checklist"
- Referral request: "Know anyone looking to buy or sell? I'd love to help them like I helped you"
