## Fulfillment Architecture

All entry-level products should be:
- ✅ **Instant delivery** (automated download/email)
- ✅ **Zero manual work** (N8N handles delivery)
- ✅ **Self-serve** (no calls, no onboarding)
- ✅ **Upsell embedded** (every product mentions next step)
- ✅ **Community-driven** (Discord for support, not email)

### N8N Automation: Product Delivery Flow

```
Payment (Stripe) → Webhook → Verify → Deliver product → 
  → Send welcome email (with Discord invite)
  → Add to "starter" email sequence
  → Track in CRM as "starter customer"
  → Tag for upsell follow-up (day 14, 30, 60)
```
