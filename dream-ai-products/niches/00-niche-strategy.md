## Pain Point Deep Dives

### 1. Real Estate Agents 💰
**Annual revenue range:** $80K–$500K+
**Who pays:** Individual agents, teams, brokerages

| Pain Point | Impact | AI Solution | Price Anchor |
|-----------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Missed leads (calls/texts after hours) | Lost deals $5K–$30K each | AI Voice Receptionist — 24/7 lead capture, qualification, appointment booking | $497/mo |
| Slow follow-up on web leads | 78% buy from first responder | AI Speed-to-Lead — instant SMS/call back within 60 seconds | $297/mo |
| CRM data entry & management | 5–10 hrs/week wasted | AI CRM Auto-Fill — extracts info from calls, fills CRM fields | $197/mo |
| Listing description writing | Time-consuming, inconsistent | AI Listing Writer — MLS-compliant descriptions in seconds | $97/mo (one-time or credit pack) |
| Client nurture after closing | 70% of clients don't get referred | AI Follow-Up Agent — drip campaigns, check-ins, referral requests | $197/mo |

**Dream AI positioning:** "Every missed call is a $15,000 commission left on the table."
