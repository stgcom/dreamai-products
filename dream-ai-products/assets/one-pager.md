### Design Notes for Production

- **Layout:** Single page, generous whitespace. This is a sales tool, not a brochure.
- **Color palette:** Dream AI brand purple (#7C3AED) as primary accent. Dark text (#1F2937) on white background.
- **Typography:** Bold headers (H2 size), body text at 10-11pt for print readability.
- **QR Code:** Link to Calendly or demo booking page. Minimum 2cm × 2cm. Include short URL underneath for manual entry.
- **Logo placement:** Top-left or center-top. Never bottom-right.
- **Footer:** Contact info, tagline, and QR code in the bottom 15% of the page.
- **What NOT to include:** Long paragraphs, jargon, feature lists without context, stock photos.
- **Print file:** Export as PDF with bleed marks. Also generate a PNG version for digital sharing.
