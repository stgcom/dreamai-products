# Dream AI — Sales Deck Script (15 Slides)

> **Product:** Dream AI — AI Employee Platform for SMEs

> **Pitch duration:** 25–35 minutes (adjust pacing to audience energy)
> **Deck philosophy:** Story-driven, not feature-tour. One idea per slide. Slides support the conversation — they don't replace it.

**Headline:** Your Customers Will Swear It's Human

**Visual direction:** Call flow diagram with audio wave visual. Show the journey: incoming call → AI answers → qualifies → routes/books → logs. Conversational interface mockup on phone screen.

**Content:**

**Key talking points:**

## SLIDE 13 — FAQ / Objection Preemption

**Headline:** Questions You're Already Thinking

**Visual direction:** FAQ cards. Conversational format — objection style. Not a boring list. Use speech bubbles or card format.

**Q: "Will my customers know it's AI?"**
Most won't. Our voice AI is designed to sound natural and conversational. But full transparency is built in — the AI identifies itself if asked, and it always transfers to a human when needed.

**Q: "What if the AI says something wrong?"**
Every call is logged and reviewed. The AI operates within your approved knowledge base. If it encounters something outside its scope, it transfers to your team with a full summary. You're always in control.

**Q: "Will this replace my receptionist?"**
Not necessarily. Most clients redeploy their front desk staff to higher-value work — in-office patient care, client-facing tasks, project management. Dream AI handles the repetitive calls so your team handles the important stuff.

**Q: "What's the setup time?"**
Under 48 hours for most businesses. We handle the heavy lifting. Your active involvement is about 10 minutes for the business profile and 30 minutes for testing and approval.

**Q: "Can I cancel anytime?"**
Yes. No long-term contracts. If it's not delivering value, you cancel at the end of your billing cycle. We're confident enough in the product to put that in writing.

**Q: "What industries does this work for?"**
Any business that gets calls and leads. We're strongest in home services, professional services, healthcare, legal, and real estate — but the platform is industry-agnostic.

**Speaker notes:**
> "I've done enough of these to know what you're thinking, so let me knock out the top questions. [Read through 3-4 most relevant to the audience.] The bottom line: you stay in control. The AI works for you, not the other way around. And if something's not right, you know about it immediately."

**Key talking points:**
- Pick 3-4 most relevant to the audience — don't read all 6 verbatim
- The "will my customers know" question is #1 for almost every prospect
- "Replace my receptionist" is a sensitive objection — handle with care, frame as augmentation
- End with the control message — reduces fear

## Appendix — Deck Delivery Notes

**For in-person presentations:**
- Use a clicker. Don't stand behind a laptop.
- Bring a portable speaker if presenting to groups (phone speakers don't cut it)
- Have the live demo pre-loaded and tested
- Print one-pagers to leave behind

**For video calls:**
- Share screen on the CRM demo slide — that's the "wow"
- Send the one-pager PDF in chat before you start
- Record the demo portion for follow-up

**For async/email send:**
- Convert to PDF with speaker notes removed
- Add a 3-sentence executive summary at the top
- Include the one-pager as a companion document
