## SECTION 8: FAQ
**<!-- HTML: <section class="faq-section"> — accordion component, each Q is <details><summary>, answer in <div class="faq-answer"> -->**

**Q: Will this sound robotic to my leads?**
No. Dream AI uses natural voice and text that sounds like a trained ISA. Most leads don't realize they're talking to AI until they're already qualified.

**Q: What if I get a lead that needs me personally?**
Hot leads get immediately escalated to your phone via call or text. You decide the qualification criteria — budget thresholds, timeline, urgency.

**Q: Does it work with my existing phone number?**
Yes. You can forward your current line, use a new number, or route calls through Dream AI as a parallel system. No number changes needed.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
You own your data. Everything exports to your CRM in standard formats. No lock-in, no hostage data.

**Q: How is this different from a virtual receptionist service?**
A human receptionist costs $300-$600/mo and works limited hours. Dream AI is 24/7, qualifies leads against your criteria, books showings, and learns your market — all for less.

**Annotations:**
- FAQ order mirrors buying journey: trust → control → logistics → risk → comparison
- Each answer is under 60 words — respects the reader's time
- Final question handles the main competitor objection head-on
