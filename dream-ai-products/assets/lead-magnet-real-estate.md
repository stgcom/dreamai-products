### What an AI Lead Receptionist Actually Does

An AI receptionist for real estate isn't a chatbot. It's not a canned voicemail greeting. It's a trained, voice-capable assistant that handles inbound leads with the same quality as your best inside sales agent — but it never sleeps, never gets sick, and never goes on vacation.

Here's the workflow:

**1. Answers Every Inbound Channel**

- Phone calls (inbound to your office or marketing number)
- Website chat inquiries
- SMS/text responses to ads
- Zillow, Realtor.com, and portal lead notifications

The AI picks up within 3 seconds. Every time. 3 AM, Christmas Day, during your kid's soccer game.

**2. Qualifies the Lead in Real Time**

The AI asks the same questions your ISA would ask, but faster and more consistently:

- Are you looking to buy, sell, or just exploring?
- What's your timeline? (Urgent, 1–3 months, just browsing)
- What area are you interested in?
- Are you pre-approved? (Buyers) / Do you have a home to sell? (Sellers)
- What's your price range?
- When's the best time for a showing or consultation?

This takes 90 seconds. The lead feels heard, served, and taken care of.

**3. Books Qualified Leads Into Your Calendar**

If a lead meets your qualification criteria — right timeline, right area, right budget — the AI books them directly into your Calendly, Google Calendar, or showing system.

You wake up to a calendar full of warm, pre-qualified appointments.

**4. Escalates When It Should**

The AI knows its limits. Complex negotiation questions, emotional calls, pricing disputes, anything requiring human judgment — the call transfers to you instantly with full context and a summary of the conversation so far.

**5. Logs Everything to Your CRM**

Every call, text, and qualification answer is automatically extracted and entered into your CRM — Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, LionDesk, HubSpot, or whatever you use. Zero data entry. Zero lost notes.
