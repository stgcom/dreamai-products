## SECTION 8: FAQ
**<!-- HTML: <section class="faq-section"> — accordion, clean medical aesthetic, easy tap targets for mobile -->**

**Q: Will my patients know they're talking to AI?**
Most don't. Dream AI uses natural conversation that matches your practice's tone. For patients who prefer a human, hot transfers go directly to your front desk.

**Q: Can it handle treatment-specific pre-care instructions?**
Yes. You configure instructions per treatment — Botox, fillers, laser, microneedling, etc. Patients receive exactly what they need, automatically, at the right time.

**Q: What if a patient has a medical question or emergency?**
Dream AI is programmed to escalate anything clinical or urgent immediately to your staff. It never provides medical advice — it follows your escalation rules precisely.

**Q: Will this integrate with Mindbody / Boulevard / my EHR?**
Dream AI integrates with Mindbody, Boulevard, Mangomint, DrChrono, and 30+ other systems. If you use something else, our API connects to most platforms.

**Q: Is this HIPAA compliant?**
Yes. Dream AI is built on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with BAA agreements available on all paid plans. Patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

**Annotations:**
- Medical escalation question placed 3rd — addresses the clinical concern that every practice owner silently worries about
- HIPAA question is last because it should be table stakes, not the headline
- Integration names in Q4 create a moment of "oh, they support my system"
