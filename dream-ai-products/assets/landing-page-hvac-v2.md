## SECTION 8: FAQ
**<!-- HTML: <section class="faq-section"> — accordion, sturdy tap targets, no-nonsense Q&A -->**

**Q: What if a customer needs to talk to a real person?**
Dream AI hot-transfers urgent calls to your on-call tech or dispatcher instantly. Customers never feel stuck — they get a human when they need one.

**Q: Will this mess up my existing dispatch workflow?**
No. Dream AI feeds into your current system (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, etc.). Jobs appear in your dispatch board exactly as if your team took the call.

**Q: Can it handle commercial calls too?**
Yes. You set routing rules by call type — residential emergencies, commercial service, installations, maintenance agreements. Different rules for different callers.

**Q: What happens during peak season when call volume triples?**
Dream AI scales automatically. No busy signals, no hold times. Every call gets handled whether it's 5 a day or 500.

**Q: Is there a contract?**
No contracts. Month-to-month. If it's not saving you money and time, cancel with one click. But after your first July, you won't want to.

**Annotations:**
- Q2 ("mess up my dispatch workflow") is the operational fear — addressed head-on and early
- Q4 (peak season scaling) is the "what about the busy times?" objection every trades business has
- Final answer ("after your first July") adds personality and seasonal relevance without being corny
