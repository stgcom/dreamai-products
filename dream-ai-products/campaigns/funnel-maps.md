# Dream AI — Funnel Blueprints

> **Revenue model:** Low-ticket digital products with built-in upsell paths.
> **All funnels assume:** Email capture at first touchpoint, automated nurture sequences, and strategic upsells.
> **Conversion rates are targets based on industry benchmarks for digital products.**

---

## FUNNEL 1: Missed Call Calculator → Email → $297 Starter Kit

The calculator is a "self-qualification" tool. Anyone who completes it has already admitted they have a revenue leak. They're primed for a solution. The $3 price removes friction — it's an impulse buy that seeds the relationship. The email sequence does the heavy lifting to $297.

## FUNNEL 3: Social Content → Profile → DM → Demo Call

### The Logic

This is the high-ticket funnel. No paid ads — pure organic + DM automation. Every touchpoint is designed to build trust and qualify intent before getting on a call. The demo call sells consulting, custom implementation, or the Starter Kit at scale. Highest AOV, lowest volume.

│  → Results screenshots / social proof                           │

## FUNNEL COMPARISON SUMMARY

| Metric | Funnel 1 (Calculator) | Funnel 2 (Scorecard) | Funnel 3 (Social/DM) |
|--------|----------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| **Traffic source** | Paid (Meta + Google) | Paid (Meta + Google + LinkedIn) | Organic (social) |
| **Entry point** | $3 Calculator | $5 Scorecard | Free content |
| **Primary upsell** | $297 Starter Kit | $17-27 Playbook → $297 Starter Kit | $297-$5K consulting |
| **Avg revenue/1K visitors** | $597-$2,303 | $401-$1,096 | $1,258-$7,755 |
| **Blended CPA** | $6-15 | $10-25 | $0 (organic) |
| **Time to optimize** | 2-4 weeks | 3-5 weeks | 4-8 weeks |
| **Best for** | Volume + quick wins | Thought leadership + B2B | High-ticket + consulting |
| **Scale ceiling** | $50K-100K/mo | $30K-70K/mo | $10K-30K/mo |

### Recommended Combination Strategy

Run all three simultaneously:
1. **Funnels 1 & 2** drive volume and revenue through paid ads
2. **Funnel 3** builds brand, authority, and high-ticket revenue organically
3. **Shared email list** — all funnels feed the same email database for cross-sell campaigns
4. **Retargeting bridges** — calculator/scorecard buyers see Starter Kit ads; social followers see scorecard ads

**Monthly revenue target at full optimization:**
- 50,000 paid visitors → $20K-100K from Funnels 1 & 2
- 10,000 organic visitors → $12K-77K from Funnel 3
- **Combined: $32K-177K/mo**

*All conversion rates are targets. Track actuals weekly and optimize. The biggest lever is email sequence performance — a 1% improvement in sequence CTR compounds across every lead.*
