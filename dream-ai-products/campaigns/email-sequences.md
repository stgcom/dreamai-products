## Sending Rules

- **Send times:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM recipient timezone
- **Spacing:** 2–3 days between emails
- **Personalization:** Always use [First Name], [Business Name], [City/Area]
- **Unsubscribe:** Include at bottom of every email
- **Tracking:** Opens, clicks, replies
- **Stop rules:** Stop sequence on reply (positive or negative)
- **Follow-up:** Positive reply → Book demo within 24h
