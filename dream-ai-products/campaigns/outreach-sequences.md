## Cold Call Script — Real Estate

### Opening (15 seconds)
```
"Hey [First Name], this is [Your Name] with Dream AI. I'll be brief — I help real estate agents recover deals they're currently losing to missed calls and slow follow-up. Do you have 60 seconds?"
```

### If YES — Qualify
```
"Cool. Quick question — when a lead calls or texts after hours, what happens right now?"

[Listen for pain]

"And roughly how many leads come in per week from Zillow, Realtor.com, your website?"

[Listen]

"The reason I'm calling — we've built an AI assistant that answers every call instantly, 24/7, qualifies the lead, and books appointments directly into your calendar or CRM. Agents using it are recovering 3–5 deals per month they'd otherwise lose.

Would it make sense to show you how it works? Takes about 15 minutes."
```

### If NO — Leave Voicemail (see below) + Follow-Up

### Key Questions to Ask
- "Who handles your leads when you're showing property or at closings?"
- "What's your average commission? What does one lost deal cost you?"
- "Are you currently using any automated follow-up tools?"

### Objection Handling — Real Estate

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **"I already have an ISA/virtual assistant."** | "That's great — an ISA is valuable. But they can't work 24/7 and they cost $3K–$5K/month. Our AI works around the clock for a fraction of that and never calls in sick. Could it complement what your ISA does?" |
| **"I handle my own leads."** | "Respect — top producers usually do. But what about after 7 PM or on weekends? That's when 40% of online leads come in. What happens then?" |
| **"I'm not interested in AI."** | "Totally fair — a lot of agents feel that way. This isn't about replacing you or your team. It's about making sure every lead gets a response, even when you're busy. Can I show you what it actually looks like in action?" |
| **"What does it cost?"** | "Plans start at $297/month. But think about it this way — if it recovers just one deal per quarter, that's $5K–$15K in commission for a $297 investment. Can I walk you through the ROI?" |
| **"I'm happy with my current system."** | "That's awesome. Quick question though — does your current system answer calls at 11 PM on a Saturday and book showings? If so, you're ahead of 90% of agents I talk to." |
| **"Send me some info."** | "Happy to. What's the best email? And so I can send you the most relevant info — what's your biggest lead gen source right now? Zillow, your website, referrals?" |
| **"I'm too busy right now."** | "I get it — you're a top producer. That's exactly why this matters. What if I could give you back 10+ hours a week? How about [specific day] at [specific time]? 15 minutes, that's it." |

## Personalization Rules
- **Always reference:** Company name, city/market, specific detail (listing count, reviews, social post, truck fleet)
- **Never:** "I hope this finds you well" / "I wanted to reach out" (be direct)
- **Always:** Lead with their pain, not your product
- **Always:** Include a specific time ask ("15 minutes" not "a call")

## Follow-Up Tracking
Log each touchpoint with:
- Date / Channel / Response status (Sent / Opened / Replied / Booked / Dead)
- Notes on objections or interest level
- Next action and scheduled date
