**Purpose:** Scenario-specific outreach for different prospect profiles.
**Tone:** Sharp, commercial, zero fluff. Every email makes inaction feel expensive.
**Structure:** Hook → Value → Proof → Low-friction CTA

## Usage Notes

1. **Personalization depth matters.** These templates work best with at least 2 levels of personalization (name + specific trigger). Level 3-4 (referencing their Zillow spend, review content, specific listing) dramatically increases reply rates.
2. **Don't stack templates.** Pick one that fits the prospect. Sending the "poor reviews" template to a 4.8-rated agent kills credibility instantly.
3. **Follow up.** Every template above should be followed with a 2-email drip (3-day gap) using the sequence framework from `cold-email-re-full.md`.
4. **Track by template.** Tag each template in your CRM so you can measure which scenarios convert best.
