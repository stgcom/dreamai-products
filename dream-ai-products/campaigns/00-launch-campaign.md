## Phase 2: Outbound Demand (Days 15–45)

### LinkedIn Campaign (VECTOR + AXIOM)

**Target:** Business owners in priority niches

| Step | Action | Timing |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Connect request + profile view | Day 1 |
| 2 | Value-first message (no pitch) | Day 3 |
| 3 | Pain-point question | Day 7 |
| 4 | Lead magnet share | Day 10 |
| 5 | Demo offer | Day 14 |

**Volume:** 50 connections/day = 1,500/month

### Cold Email (AXIOM)

**Sequence:** 5 emails over 14 days

| Email | Subject Line | Purpose |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | "Quick question about [niche] lead response" | Open, establish problem |
| 2 | "The stat that made me start Dream AI" | Share insight, build authority |
| 3 | "How [competitor/similar business] stopped losing leads" | Case study / social proof |
| 4 | "I built something you can try for free" | Lead magnet offer |
| 5 | "Still interested in never missing a lead?" | Break-up + demo CTA |

**Volume:** 200/day across niches

### Direct Outreach (AXIOM)
- Google Maps scraping for target niches (name, phone, website)
- Phone number → AI Voice demo call
- "Hey, this is an AI employee demo — we just called your business and you didn't answer. Want to see how we'd capture that lead?"

---

## Phase 3: Conversion & Social Proof (Days 45–75)

### Pilot Program
- 3–5 free/pilot clients per niche
- Full deployment + case study capture
- Testimonials + screenshots + results data

### Webinar / Live Demo
- "Watch an AI Employee Take 5 Calls in Real Time"
- Live demo of agent handling leads
- Q&A + limited-time offer

### Case Studies
- Real estate agent: "AI captured 47 leads in 30 days that would have been missed"
- MedSpa: "Reduced no-shows by 68% with AI appointment agent"
- HVAC: "Booked $23,000 in emergency jobs after hours"

---

## Phase 4: Scale (Days 75–90)

### Referral Engine
- Existing clients → "Refer a business, get 1 month free"
- Affiliate program for consultants/agents in each niche

### Paid Ads (once unit economics proven)
- Facebook/Instagram: Retarget quiz takers + webinar attendees
- Google Ads: "[niche] AI assistant", "AI receptionist for [niche]"
- Budget: $1,000–$3,000/month initially

### Productized Self-Serve
- Launch "AI Employee Starter Kit" ($297) as self-serve product
- Pre-built agent + video training + community access
- Upsell to managed service ($497+/mo)
