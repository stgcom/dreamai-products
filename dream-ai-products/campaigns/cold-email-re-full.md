## A/B Testing Matrix

| Email | A Variant | B Variant | Test Variable |
|-------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | "missed calls last week" | "[City] agents losing deals" | Data vs. local personalization |
| 2 | "2:17 AM" | "when leads call after hours" | Specificity vs. clarity |
| 3 | "what the top 5% know" | "your next competitor just automated" | Aspiration vs. competitive pressure |
| 4 | "your CRM is a graveyard" | "1,200 contacts, zero follow-up" | Metaphor vs. specificity |
| 5 | "closing this thread" | "last one from me" | Directness vs. casual |

**Testing protocol:** Send A to 50%, B to 50%. Run for 5 days minimum before declaring a winner. Track open rate and reply rate separately.
