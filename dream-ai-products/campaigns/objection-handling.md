# Dream AI — Objection Handling Playbook

> **Owner:** PULSE (Client Success & Expansion Lead) with input from AXIOM (Sales Intelligence)
> **Purpose:** Scripted responses to the 20 most common objections per niche, enabling consistent, high-conversion conversations.

---

## Objection Handling Framework

### The A.C.E. Method

Every objection gets handled in three steps:

1. **Acknowledge** — Validate their concern. Don't dismiss or argue.
2. **Clarify** — Reframe with context, data, or a question that reveals the real objection.
3. **Evidence** — Provide proof (case study, demo, metric, guarantee) that resolves the concern.

### Rules of Engagement

- Never say "I understand" robotically — *demonstrate* understanding by restating their specific concern
- Use their language back to them
- Every response ends with a question (keeps the conversation moving)
- If the objection is a deal-breaker (wrong fit), say so honestly — protects NPS long-term
- Track objections in CRM — patterns inform product and marketing decisions

## Niche 1: Coaches & Consultants

### 1. "I can do this myself with ChatGPT"

**Acknowledge:** "Totally fair — ChatGPT is a great tool for general content."
**Clarify:** "The question is: does it know your methodology, your client's specific pain points, and how to position your offer? Generic content doesn't convert."
**Evidence:** "We built [Client Name]'s entire content engine on their proprietary framework. They went from posting randomly to generating [X] qualified leads per month. Want me to show you how?"

### 2. "My clients are too niche for AI to understand"

**Acknowledge:** "Niche expertise is exactly why generic tools fail."
**Clarify:** "What if the AI was trained specifically on *your* methodology, *your* voice, and *your* client profiles?"
**Evidence:** "We've worked with [niche coach example] who had the same concern. Within 30 days, their content sounded more like them than their actual VA did. Want to see the before/after?"

### 3. "I don't have time to set this up"

**Acknowledge:** "That's the irony — you're too busy doing the thing that AI could speed up."
**Clarify:** "How much time per week are you spending on content, follow-ups, or admin right now?"
**Evidence:** "Our average client saves 10+ hours/week within the first month. Setup takes 30 minutes on our kickoff call. We do the heavy lifting. Fair to give it 30 minutes to save 10 hours a week?"

### 4. "I've tried AI tools before and they were mediocre"

**Acknowledge:** "Most AI tools *are* mediocre. That's a fair concern."
**Clarify:** "What specifically disappointed you? Was it the voice, the quality, or the strategy behind it?"
**Evidence:** "Dream AI isn't a generic tool — it's a system built around *your* business. [Client name] tried 3 tools before us. Their first week with us produced content their audience actually engaged with. Want to see why?"

### 5. "It's too expensive for where I am in my business"

**Acknowledge:** "Budget discipline is important. I respect that."
**Clarify:** "Let me ask — what's the cost of *not* systemizing your client acquisition right now? How many clients are you leaving on the table because your content and follow-up are inconsistent?"
**Evidence:** "Most of our coaching clients recoup the investment with one new client. At [price], that's [X] clients. Is that realistic for your practice?"

### 6. "What if my audience can tell it's AI?"

**Acknowledge:** "That's a smart concern — authenticity matters more than anything in coaching."
**Clarify:** "The goal isn't to replace you. It's to amplify you. Would your audience know if a really good copywriter wrote your emails?"
**Evidence:** "We clone your voice, your stories, your frameworks. [Client] gets compliments on her emails weekly — and her team knows AI helped draft them. It's *you*, just faster."

### 7. "I need to think about it"

**Acknowledge:** "Of course. This is a real decision."
**Clarify:** "Help me understand — is it the investment, the fit, or something else?"
**Evidence:** "Here's what I'd suggest: let's do a quick pilot. I'll show you what your content looks like with Dream AI, zero obligation. If it's not a fit, we part as friends. Fair?"

### 8. "My business is different — I don't do typical marketing"

**Acknowledge:** "You're right — relationship-based businesses play by different rules."
**Clarify:** "What does your ideal client acquisition process actually look like right now?"
**Evidence:** "Dream AI isn't a one-size-fits-all marketing machine. We adapt to *your* process. [Client] runs a referral-based practice and uses us specifically for nurturing and positioning — not cold outreach. Want to see how that works?"

## Niche 2: Small Business Owners (Local/Service)

### 9. "I'm not tech-savvy"

**Acknowledge:** "You don't need to be. That's literally our job."
**Clarify:** "If everything ran in the background and you just saw results — leads, bookings, reviews — would that work for you?"
**Evidence:** "[Client] runs a [business type] and can barely use email. Their AI system handles 80% of customer follow-up automatically. They just show up to close the deal."

### 10. "I get all my business from word of mouth"

**Acknowledge:** "That's a strong foundation. Word of mouth is the best channel."
**Clarify:** "What happens when word of mouth slows down? Or what if you could systematize it so every happy customer automatically refers two more?"
**Evidence:** "We helped [business] turn their word-of-mouth machine into a systematic referral engine. Same great reputation, just automated. They went from [metric] to [metric]."

### 11. "I already have a website/person doing my marketing"

**Acknowledge:** "Great — you're already invested in marketing. This makes it better."
**Clarify:** "How fast does your current person or website respond to leads? What's the follow-up like after someone inquires?"
**Evidence:** "Dream AI works *with* your existing setup. We handle the part most businesses neglect: follow-up, nurturing, and content consistency. [Client] had a web designer and still doubled leads because we automated what happened *after* the website."

### 12. "AI will replace my personal touch"

**Acknowledge:** "The personal touch is what makes your business special. Nobody wants to lose that."
**Clarify:** "What if AI handled the *volume* parts — booking confirmations, follow-ups, review requests — so you could spend *more* time on the personal interactions that matter?"
**Evidence:** "We're not replacing you. We're removing the busywork so you can be more personal, not less. [Client] uses us for automated follow-up and their customer satisfaction scores actually went *up*."

### 13. "I can't afford another monthly subscription"

**Acknowledge:** "Every dollar matters when you're running a small business."
**Clarify:** "How many leads or customers are you losing each month because follow-up falls through the cracks?"
**Evidence:** "Most of our small business clients pick up 2–5 extra jobs per month from our system. At [average job value], that's [revenue] against [monthly cost]. When did it pay for itself — week one or week two?"

### 14. "What if it says something wrong to my customers?"

**Acknowledge:** "You should absolutely be concerned about that. Reputation is everything."
**Clarify:** "Would it help if nothing went out without your approval until you're comfortable?"
**Evidence:** "Every AI communication starts as a draft or requires your sign-off. [Client] reviews for the first two weeks, then trusts it completely. You're always in control."

### 15. "My customers are older / not online"

**Acknowledge:** "Know your customer. That's good business sense."
**Clarify:** "What channels *do* they respond to? Phone? Text? Mail?"
**Evidence:** "We work with businesses whose average customer is 60+. Text messages have 98% open rates across all age groups. [Client] sends automated appointment reminders via text and no-shows dropped by 40%."

### 16. "I've been burned by marketing agencies before"

**Acknowledge:** "That's unfortunately common. The agency model has problems."
**Clarify:** "What specifically went wrong? Lack of results, lack of communication, or being locked into a long contract?"
**Evidence:** "We're the opposite of a typical agency. No long contracts. You own everything. We report results weekly. And you can fire us any time. Want to see exactly what [client] got in their first 30 days?"

## Niche 3: E-Commerce & DTC Brands

### 17. "Our funnel already converts — we don't need this"

**Acknowledge:** "If your funnel is working, that's a great position to be in."
**Clarify:** "What's your customer reactivation rate? How many one-time buyers come back for a second purchase?"
**Evidence:** "Dream AI isn't about replacing your funnel. It's about what happens after the sale — post-purchase nurture, review generation, and repeat purchase campaigns. [Brand] saw a 35% increase in LTV from our post-purchase system alone."

### 18. "We already have Klaviyo/Mailchimp/our email platform"

**Acknowledge:** "Those are solid platforms. We're not asking you to replace them."
**Clarify:** "Are you using them to their full potential? How many segments are you actively running? How personalized is your content?"
**Evidence:** "Dream AI layers on top of your existing platform. We generate the strategy, copy, and segments that most teams don't have bandwidth to create. [Brand] kept Klaviyo and tripled their email revenue because we made the content actually good."

### 19. "AI-generated product descriptions? That sounds generic"

**Acknowledge:** "You're right — most AI descriptions are forgettable."
**Clarify:** "What if every description matched your brand voice, highlighted your USP, and was optimized for the specific channel — Amazon, Shopify, TikTok Shop?"
**Evidence:** "[Brand] in your space used us for 500 product descriptions. Their conversion rate on Shopify went from [X]% to [Y]%. Not because of generic copy — because of *strategic* AI copy that actually sells."

### 20. "We're focused on paid ads, not organic"

**Acknowledge:** "Paid ads are a strong acquisition play. No argument there."
**Clarify:** "What's your current blended CAC? And what happens to your brand when you turn ads off?"
**Evidence:** "The brands we work with use paid for acquisition and Dream AI for retention and organic. [Brand] cut their ad spend by 20% because organic content started driving direct traffic. What would 20% of your ad budget back in your pocket look like?"

## Universal Objections (All Niches)

### 21. "This sounds too good to be true"

**Acknowledge:** "Healthy skepticism is smart."
**Clarify:** "What would you need to see to believe it works?"
**Evidence:** "I can show you live dashboards, real client results, or connect you with a client in your niche. What's most convincing to you?"

### 22. "I need to talk to my partner/co-founder/team"

**Acknowledge:** "Absolutely — important decisions involve the right people."
**Clarify:** "What concerns do you think they'll have? I can address those proactively."
**Evidence:** "Let me put together a one-page summary you can share. And if they want to jump on a quick call, I'm available this week."

### 23. "What's the contract / can I cancel?"

**Acknowledge:** "You should know exactly what you're signing up for."
**Clarify:** "Is it the commitment length or the cancellation process that concerns you?"
**Evidence:** "No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. We earn your business every month. [Client] has been with us for [X] months — not because they're locked in, but because it works."

### 24. "I'm in a slow season"

**Acknowledge:** "Seasonality is real. Smart to plan around it."
**Clarify:** "Is slow season a reason to go dark, or the perfect time to build systems so you're ready when demand spikes?"
**Evidence:** "[Client] onboarded during their slowest month and had their best Q4 ever because the system was ready to go. What if this slow season becomes your setup for the next big one?"

### 25. "How do I know this will work for *my* business?"

**Acknowledge:** "Fair question. No one-size-fits-all answer."
**Clarify:** "Tell me about your best customer. What made them buy? What made them come back?"
**Evidence:** "That's exactly what we build around. We don't use templates — we build your system from your actual customer behavior. Give us 30 days and a real chance. If it doesn't work, you haven't lost anything."

## Objection Tracking (Internal)

Log every objection in CRM with:
- **Date**
- **Client/prospect name**
- **Objection category** (price / trust / time / fit / other)
- **Response used**
- **Outcome** (resolved / escalated / deal lost)
- **Notes** (what worked, what didn't)

### Monthly Objection Review
- [ ] Pull top 5 objections by frequency
- [ ] Identify if any are becoming patterns (product problem vs. sales problem)
- [ ] Update playbook with new responses
- [ ] Share learnings with marketing (content that addresses top objections)

## Quick Reference: Objection Cheat Sheet

| Objection Category | Core Response Theme |
|---|---|
| **Price** | ROI framing — cost of *not* acting vs. investment |
| **"I can do it myself"** | Amplification, not replacement; scale + consistency |
| **"Tried AI before"** | Differentiate: this is custom, not generic |
| **"No time"** | Time *saved* vs. time *spent*; setup is done for them |
| **"Not tech-savvy"** | Done-for-you; results you can see without understanding tech |
| **"Already have tools"** | Layer on top; enhance what you have |
| **"Think about it"** | Diagnose the real concern; offer a no-risk pilot |
| **"Wrong niche/audience"** | Custom approach; show niche-specific examples |
| **"Burned before"** | Transparency, no contracts, real results, easy exit |
| **"Need to ask partner"** | Equip them with the pitch; offer to join the call |
