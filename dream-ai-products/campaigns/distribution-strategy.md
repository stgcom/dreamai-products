#### Template C — HVAC (Pain-Forward)

> Hi {{first_name}}, I help small business owners in {{industry}} capture more leads with AI assistants that work 24/7. Would love to connect and exchange ideas.

**Rules:**

- No pitch in connection request — curiosity only

### 1.3 Messaging Sequence (Post-Connection)

#### Sequence Structure: 5 messages over 21 days

| Step | Day | Type | Purpose |
|------|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | +1 | Value message | Share a relevant stat or insight (no pitch) |
| 2 | +4 | Pain question | Ask about their specific pain point |
| 3 | +8 | Social proof | Share a mini case study or result |
| 4 | +14 | Lead magnet | Offer the free playbook / quiz |
| 5 | +21 | Demo CTA | Direct ask for a 15-min demo call |

---

**Real Estate version:**
> Hey {{first_name}}, just came across this stat — 78% of homebuyers go with the first agent to respond to their inquiry. Wild how much business disappears just from not picking up fast enough. Curious how you handle inbound leads at {{company_name}}?

### 2.2 Joining & Engagement Rules

1. **Join 3–5 groups per niche** (max 15 groups total to manage quality)
2. **First 2 weeks: 100% value** — answer questions, comment helpfully, build rapport
3. **Never join and immediately post** — engage on others' posts first
4. **Profile optimization:** Headline = "I help [niche] owners capture leads with AI 24/7 | Dream AI" + professional photo + cover image with value proposition

### 2.3 Posting Tactics & Content Calendar

#### Post Types (rotate weekly)

**Type 1 — Problem Post (get engagement)**
> "Real estate agents — what's your biggest lead source right now? And how many leads do you think you lose per week to missed calls/texts?"
>
> *Follow-up in comments with value, then DM the most engaged commenters*

**Type 2 — Micro Case Study (build credibility)**
> "Shared this with a MedSpa owner last week and she almost cried:
>
> She was losing ~$3,200/month in no-shows. We set up an AI appointment confirmation system — texts patients 24hrs before + morning of. No-show rate dropped from 28% to 9% in 30 days.
>
> That's $2,300/month recovered. For a $297/mo tool.
>
> Anyone else dealing with no-shows?"

**Type 3 — Contrarian/Thought Leadership**
> "Hot take: The #1 tool every HVAC contractor needs isn't a new truck or better equipment.
>
> It's someone (or something) answering the phone at 2 AM when a homeowner's furnace dies.
>
> Because that homeowner is calling the next number on Google. And it's not yours."

**Type 4 — Poll (quick engagement)**
> "Quick poll for real estate agents:
>
> 🔹 How do you handle leads that come in after hours?
> A) Call them back the next morning
> B) I'm always available (I never sleep)
> C) They go to my voicemail
> D) I have no idea"

**Type 5 — Behind-the-Scenes / Demo**
> "Made a 30-second video of an AI agent booking an appointment for a MedSpa. No human involved.
>
> [Video/GIF]
>
> This is what the front desk of the future looks like. Happy to share how it works if anyone's curious."

#### Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Post Type | Niche Focus |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| Monday | Problem Post / Question | Rotate niches |
| Tuesday | Engage only (comments on others' posts) | — |
| Wednesday | Micro Case Study / Results | Highest-priority niche |
| Thursday | Engage only | — |
| Friday | Poll or Thought Leadership | Broad/small biz |
| Saturday | Behind-the-Scenes / Demo | Any |

### 2.4 DM Follow-Up Sequence (from Group Engagement)

When someone comments or reacts to a post:

> **Message 1 (within 1 hour):**
> "Hey {{first_name}}! Thanks for the comment on that post. I noticed you're in {{niche}} — are you dealing with [specific pain point] at your business too?"

> **Message 2 (if they reply positively):**
> "Cool — I actually have a free playbook that covers exactly how to solve that. Want me to send it over? It's a quick read, no sales pitch."

> **Message 3 (after they get the playbook):**
> "Hope that was useful! If you want to see how it works in practice, I can do a quick 15-min live demo. Just show you what the AI actually does. Interested?"
