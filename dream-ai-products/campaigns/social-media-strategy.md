## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Pain Awareness (30% of content)
**Goal:** Create urgency around the problem
**Hooks:**
- "Your phone just rang at 9 PM. It was a $5,000 deal. You missed it."

- "37% of your calls come after 5 PM. Your office is closed."

- "Set up this SMS auto-reply in 5 minutes. Save 3 missed leads this week."

**Goal:** Show results, build credibility

- "A Dallas plumber installed AI receptionist. First month: $4,200 recovered."

- "Before/after: 3 hours on the phone → 3 minutes reviewing AI-booked appointments."
- "Real estate team of 3 → AI handles 80% of inbound. They just close deals."

### Pillar 4: Product Teasers (15% of content)
**Goal:** Drive product purchases
**Hooks:**
- "I built a free calculator that shows your exact missed revenue. Link in bio."
- "15 questions. 60 seconds. Your AI readiness score. (Free)"
- "25 scripts that turn missed calls into booked appointments. $12."
- "This $7 SMS template replaces your entire booking dance."
- "New: The AI Employee Starter Pack"

### Pillar 5: Authority (10% of content)
**Goal:** Position as thought leader
**Hooks:**
- "AI won't replace you. But a business using AI will."
- "The 3 automations every small business needs in 2026."
- "Why 'just hire a receptionist' is bad advice (the math)."
- "What the top 1% of real estate agents do differently with technology."
- "The hidden cost of doing everything yourself."

## Organic Growth Playbook

### LinkedIn Tactics
- **Post daily** between 7-9 AM local time of target audience
- **First line = everything** — make it scroll-stopping
- **Comment on 20 posts/day** in your niche (real value, not spam)
- **Connect with 20 targeted prospects/day** (agents, spa owners, contractors)
- **Use documents** — upload carousel PDFs for 5x reach
- **Tag people** in comments when relevant (ask their opinion)
- **Repurpose:** every long post becomes a carousel, every carousel becomes 5 tweets

### Facebook Group Strategy
- **Join 20-30 niche groups** (real estate, MedSpa business, HVAC contractors)
- **Answer questions** for 2 weeks before posting promotional content
- **Post polls** — highest engagement in business groups
- **Share quick wins** with screenshots (not just text)
- **DM strategy:** anyone who engages → friend request → value DM → product mention

### Instagram Tactics
- **Reels > everything** — algorithm favors video
- **Post 3-5 Stories daily** (polls, quizzes, countdowns, behind-the-scenes)
- **Hashtag sets per niche:**
  - Real Estate: #RealEstateAgent #RealEstateTech #RealEstateLife #AgentLife #Realtor
  - MedSpa: #MedSpa #MedSpaOwner #MedicalSpa #MedSpaLife #AestheticsBusiness
  - HVAC: #HVAC #HVACContractor #HVACBusiness #HVACLife #ServiceBusiness
  - General: #SmallBusinessTips #BusinessAutomation #AIEmployee #AIForBusiness
- **DM automation:** Story mention → auto-DM with free tool link
- **Collaborate** with niche micro-influencers (cross-posts)

### TikTok Approach
- **Hook in first 2 seconds** — bold statement or visual
- **Face-to-camera** performs best for B2B
- **Trending sounds** + business content = viral potential
- **Post 1-2x/day** (volume matters more than polish)
- **Repurpose Reels** to TikTok (remove watermark, re-upload)

### YouTube Shorts
- **Repurpose best Reels/TikToks**
- **SEO titles:** "How to [solve problem] for [niche]" format
- **Post 3x/week**
- **Link to free tools in description**

## 3 Funnel Blueprints

### Funnel 1: Calculator → Starter Kit (High Volume)
```
Facebook/LinkedIn Ad (pain stat)
  → Calculator Landing Page (free tool)
    → Email Capture (get your results)
      → Day 1: Results email + urgency
      → Day 2: "The full fix" (Starter Kit intro)
      → Day 3: Case study
      → Day 5: Starter Kit offer ($297)
        → Checkout → Upsell: Pro ($497)
```
**Target:** 5% calc completion, 3% email capture, 10% email→purchase
**Revenue per 1,000 visitors:** $597–$2,303

### Funnel 2: Scorecard → Playbook (Nurture)
```
Ad → Scorecard Quiz (15 questions)
  → Results Page (score + action plan)
    → Email Capture
      → Day 0: Full report
      → Day 2: Weakest category deep-dive
      → Day 5: Quick win (earn trust)
      → Day 8: Case study
      → Day 12: Playbook offer ($17-27)
        → Upsell: Starter Kit ($297)
```
**Target:** 8% completion, 5% email capture, 8% purchase
**Revenue per 1,000 visitors:** $401–$1,096

### Funnel 3: Organic → Demo (High Value)
```
Organic social content (daily)
  → Profile follow / DM
    → DM automation: "Want the free calculator?"
      → Calculator → Email capture
        → Nurture sequence → Demo booking
          → Sales call → Starter Kit ($297) or Custom ($1,497-$5K)
```
**Target:** 1% follower→demo, 25% demo→close
**Revenue per 1,000 followers:** $1,258–$7,755 (zero ad spend)
