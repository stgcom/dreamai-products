## 1. REAL ESTATE AGENTS

### Qualification Scorecard

| Criteria | Points | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Team size:** Solo (1), 2–5 (2), 6–15 (3), 15+ (4) | /4 | Larger teams = higher ACV |
| **Active listings:** 1–5 (1), 6–15 (2), 16–30 (3), 30+ (4) | /4 | Volume = missed-call volume |
| **Transaction volume (past 12mo):** Under $5M (1), $5–20M (2), $20–50M (3), $50M+ (4) | /4 | Revenue = willingness to pay |
| **Online presence:** No website (1), Basic site (2), Active socials + IDX (3), Paid ads running (4) | /4 | Lead gen = lead follow-up pain |
| **Team members on LinkedIn:** 0 (0), 1–3 (1), 4–10 (2), 10+ (3) | /3 | Org size signal |
| **CRM visible (KvCORE, Follow Up Boss, etc.):** None (0), Mentioned in profile (2), Active posting about it (3) | /3 | Tech-forward = faster adopter |
| **Posted about pain points:** No (0), Generic business (1), Specifically about time/leads/staff (3) | /3 | Pain signal |
| **Competitor AI usage:** Unknown (0), Using Ylopo/Lofty/Rex (2), Mentioned AI tools (3) | /3 | Competitive pressure |

**Scoring Guide:**
- **24–28:** 🔴 Hot — Lead with urgency, fast-track to demo
- **16–23:** 🟡 Warm — Build relationship, nurture to pain point
- **0–15:** 🟢 Cold — Long-term nurture, add to drip

### Pain Discovery Checklist — Real Estate

- [ ] Do you miss calls from leads? (Check: do they have a team handling phones or is it just them?)
- [ ] How many leads come in weekly from Zillow/Realtor.com/website?
- [ ] Do you follow up with every lead within 5 minutes?
- [ ] How many hours/week on CRM data entry?
- [ ] Who writes your listing descriptions?
- [ ] Are past clients still in your database? Do you stay in touch?
- [ ] Do you have after-hours coverage for inquiries?
- [ ] What's your cost per lead? What percentage actually convert?
- [ ] Do you do any follow-up automations now? (Mailchimp, KVCore drip, etc.)
- [ ] How do you generate referrals from past clients?

### BANT Qualification Questions — Real Estate

**Budget:**
1. "What do you currently spend per month on lead generation tools and services?"
2. "If a tool could recover even 2–3 missed deals per year, what would that be worth to your business?"
3. "Does your brokerage provide any tech budget, or is that coming out of your commission?"

**Authority:**
1. "Are you the decision-maker for your team's tech stack, or do you work with an admin/office manager?"
2. "Who else would need to weigh in before adopting a new tool?"
3. "Are you an independent agent or under a brokerage with approved vendors?"

**Need:**
1. "What's the most painful part of your lead follow-up process right now?"
2. "How many leads do you estimate you lose every month to slow response time?"
3. "What happens when a lead calls at 9 PM on a Saturday?"
4. "If you could automate one repetitive task off your plate, what would it be?"

**Timeline:**
1. "Are you actively looking for a solution, or is this something you're exploring?"
2. "When does your current CRM/tech contract renew?"
3. "Are you in a growth phase right now, or trying to optimize what you have?"
4. "What would need to happen for you to make a change this quarter?"

### Research Sources — Real Estate
- **LinkedIn:** Search by title + brokerage name. Look for team leaders, not solo agents with no volume.
- **Zillow/Realtor.com:** Check active listings count, recent sales.
- **Local MLS stats:** Google "[city] real estate agent rankings."
- **Instagram/Facebook:** Many agents are active here — check posting frequency, ad spend signals.
- **Brokerage websites:** Check team pages for org size.

## 2. MEDSPAS

### Qualification Scorecard

| Criteria | Points | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Revenue range:** Under $500K (1), $500K–$1M (2), $1–3M (3), $3M+ (4) | /4 | Budget capacity |
| **Number of treatment rooms:** 1–2 (1), 3–5 (2), 6–10 (3), 10+ (4) | /4 | Volume = scheduling pain |
| **Staff count (non-admin):** 1–2 (1), 3–5 (2), 6–10 (3), 10+ (4) | /4 | Complexity = need for automation |
| **Online booking available:** No (0), Basic (1), Full online system (2), Online + phone booking (3) | /3 | Booking pain indicator |
| **Review rating (Google):** <4.0 (1), 4.0–4.5 (2), 4.5–4.8 (3), 4.8+ (3) | /3 | Reputation-conscious = will pay for review tools |
| **Number of Google reviews:** <50 (1), 50–200 (2), 200–500 (3), 500+ (3) | /3 | Volume = volume of patient interactions |
| **Social media presence:** None (0), Basic (1), Active/branded (2), Running paid ads (3) | /3 | Marketing investment = growth mindset |
| **Website quality:** No site (0), Basic (1), Professional w/ procedures (2), E-commerce + booking (3) | /3 | Tech sophistication |
| **Posted about staffing challenges:** No (0), Generic (1), Specifically about front desk/hiring (3) | /3 | Direct pain signal |

**Scoring Guide:**
- **27–33:** 🔴 Hot — Book demo within 48 hours
- **18–26:** 🟡 Warm — Personalized outreach, address specific pain
- **0–17:** 🟢 Cold — Nurture with case studies and content

### Pain Discovery Checklist — MedSpa

- [ ] What's your current no-show rate? (Industry avg: 20–30%)
- [ ] How are appointment confirmations handled? (Manual, automated, or not at all?)
- [ ] How many calls does your front desk handle daily?
- [ ] Do patients call with pre/post-care questions after consultations?
- [ ] How are new patient inquiries handled? (Form, phone, DMs, walk-in?)
- [ ] What's your average time from inquiry to booked consultation?
- [ ] How do you currently collect reviews?
- [ ] Do you have a waitlist for popular treatments?
- [ ] How do you handle after-hours inquiries?
- [ ] What's your patient rebooking rate? (How many come back for follow-ups?)

### BANT Qualification Questions — MedSpa

**Budget:**
1. "What does your front desk staffing cost you monthly — including salary, benefits, training?"
2. "If you could automate 60% of front desk calls, what would that free up in terms of dollar value?"
3. "What's your average revenue per patient? What's the cost of losing one to a missed call?"

**Authority:**
1. "Are you the owner, or do you work with a practice manager who handles operations?"
2. "Who decides on technology purchases here?"
3. "Is your medical director involved in operational decisions, or purely clinical?"

**Need:**
1. "What's the single biggest bottleneck in your patient flow right now?"
2. "How much staff time goes to answering the same questions repeatedly?"
3. "What happens when someone calls after hours wanting to book Botox?"
4. "Do you struggle with no-shows for consultations or treatments?"

**Timeline:**
1. "Are you currently evaluating any solutions for this?"
2. "When do you expect to open your next location / expand?"
3. "Is this a 'nice to have' or something you need solved this quarter?"
4. "What would a win look like in the first 90 days?"

### Research Sources — MedSpa
- **Google Maps:** Search "[city] medspa" — check reviews, photos, location count.
- **RealSelf:** Procedure-specific reviews and provider profiles.
- **Instagram/TikTok:** Most MedSpas are active — count followers, check if they show the space.
- **State medical board:** Verify licensing, check for multi-location ownership.
- **Google Ads Transparency Center:** Check if they're running paid ads.
- **Yelp:** Check for hidden pain signals in negative reviews (long wait times, phone unanswered).

## 3. HVAC CONTRACTORS

### Qualification Scorecard

| Criteria | Points | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Annual revenue:** Under $500K (1), $500K–$1M (2), $1–3M (3), $3M+ (4) | /4 | Budget indicator |
| **Truck count (visible on Google/socials):** 1–3 (1), 4–10 (2), 10–20 (3), 20+ (4) | /4 | Operation scale |
| **Tech count (crew size):** 1–3 (1), 4–8 (2), 9–20 (3), 20+ (4) | /4 | Dispatch complexity |
| **Service area:** Single neighborhood (1), City-wide (2), Multi-city (3), State/region (4) | /4 | Geographic coverage = call volume |
| **Google review count:** <30 (1), 30–100 (2), 100–300 (3), 300+ (3) | /3 | Customer interaction volume |
| **Has online booking:** No (0), Basic form (1), Full scheduling (2) | /2 | Tech adoption indicator |
| **24/7 or emergency service advertised:** No (0), Yes, mentioned online (3) | /3 | Direct match to after-hours AI |
| **Posted hiring ads (Indeed, Craigslist):** No (0), Dispatcher/admin (2), Techs (1) | /2 | Staffing pain = operational pain |
| **Website quality:** None (0), Basic brochure (1), SEO-optimized (2), Blog + resources (3) | /3 | Marketing investment |
| **Social media active:** No (0), Sporadic (1), Consistent (2), Running promos/ads (3) | /3 | Growth-oriented |

**Scoring Guide:**
- **27–33:** 🔴 Hot — Lead with emergency/dispatching angle
- **18–26:** 🟡 Warm — Focus on booking overflow + retention
- **0–17:** 🟢 Cold — Education on AI, build awareness

### Pain Discovery Checklist — HVAC

- [ ] How many calls does your team handle per day? (Peak season vs. off-season)
- [ ] What happens to after-hours calls? (Voicemail, answering service, competitor?)
- [ ] Do you have a dedicated dispatcher, or does the owner handle it?
- [ ] What's your average time from call to scheduled appointment?
- [ ] Do you lose jobs to faster-responding competitors?
- [ ] How do you handle seasonal overflow? (Spring/summer AC, fall/winter heating)
- [ ] What's your maintenance agreement / club membership signup rate?
- [ ] How many current customers haven't rebooked in 12+ months?
- [ ] What's your Google review response time? Do you respond to all reviews?
- [ ] What software do you use for dispatching/scheduling? (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, manual?)

### BANT Qualification Questions — HVAC

**Budget:**
1. "What does your current answering service or dispatch setup cost per month?"
2. "If you could recover 5–10 emergency calls per month that currently go to voicemail, what would that be worth?"
3. "What's an average ticket for an emergency call? And how many do you estimate you lose after hours?"

**Authority:**
1. "Are you the owner, or do you work with an office manager/dispatch coordinator?"
2. "Who handles scheduling and dispatch decisions day-to-day?"
3. "If you have multiple locations, who makes tech decisions across them?"

**Need:**
1. "What's your biggest challenge during peak season?"
2. "How many calls go to voicemail or get missed on a busy day?"
3. "Do customers complain about slow response times?"
4. "How are you currently handling after-hours emergencies?"
5. "What happens to maintenance plan customers who haven't called in a year?"

**Timeline:**
1. "Are you looking to solve this before the next busy season?"
2. "What's driving the urgency — growth, staffing issues, customer complaints?"
3. "When does your current dispatch/scheduling software contract renew?"
4. "Would you want this running before summer rush (if applicable)?"

### Research Sources — HVAC
- **Google Maps:** Search "[city] HVAC" — fleet size visible from street view, review volume.
- **ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro directories:** Some publish contractor profiles.
- **Indeed / Job boards:** Check if they're hiring dispatchers or admin staff.
- **BBB / Angi / HomeAdvisor:** Company profiles, complaint patterns.
- **Social media:** Many HVAC companies run promotions — check Facebook for ad activity.
- **State contractor license databases:** Verify and find owner names.

## UNIVERSICAL RESEARCH WORKFLOW (All Niches)

### Pre-Outreach Checklist (5 min per prospect)
- [ ] **LinkedIn profile:** Name, title, company, mutual connections, recent posts
- [ ] **Google search:** "[Company name] + [city]" — find website, reviews, socials
- [ ] **Review scan:** Quick skim of 1-star and 5-star reviews for pain/success signals
- [ ] **Website check:** Do they have online booking? What tools do they mention?
- [ ] **Social media:** Active? How many followers? Running ads?
- [ ] **Tech stack:** Can you identify any current software? (ServiceTitan, Follow Up Boss, MindBody, etc.)
- [ ] **Revenue indicators:** Team size, locations, truck count, listings volume
- [ ] **Pain signals:** Job postings, negative reviews, complaints on social, "we're hiring" posts

### Prospect Tagging System
Use these tags in your CRM/Notion:
- **[HOT-BUDGET]** — Confirmed budget + need + timeline
- **[HOT-PAIN]** — Express pain, need qualification on budget/authority
- **[WARM-REL]** — Building relationship, no immediate need
- **[COLD-NURTURE]** — Add to long-term drip
- **[COMPETITOR]** — Using competitor's solution — displacement opportunity
- **[REFERRAL]** — Came from existing customer/partner referral
- **[EVENT]** — Met at conference, trade show, or networking event
