# Dream AI — Client Onboarding Playbook (Day 1–30)

> **Owner:** PULSE (Client Success & Expansion Lead)
> **Purpose:** Structured 30-day onboarding to drive activation, reduce time-to-value, and set up long-term retention.

---

## Onboarding Philosophy

- **Day 1 magic** — Client should see value within 24 hours of purchase.
- **Guided, not dumped** — Don't hand over everything at once. Stage the rollout.
- **Checklist-driven** — Both internal team and client have clear actions at every step.
- **Prove ROI by Day 14** — If they haven't seen measurable results in two weeks, we're behind.

---

## Day 1: Welcome & Activation

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Log client in CRM with: plan tier, niche, start date, key goals from sales call
- [ ] Assign onboarding owner (success rep)
- [ ] Send Slack/internal notification: "New client onboarded — [name]"
- [ ] Review sales notes for custom promises or scope additions

### Client-Facing Actions
- [ ] **Welcome Email** sent within 1 hour of purchase
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Login credentials / dashboard access delivered
- [ ] "Quick Win" guide sent — one action they can do *right now* to see value

### Welcome Email Template

> **Subject:** Welcome to Dream AI — Let's Build Something Unstoppable
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> You're in. Welcome to Dream AI.
>
> Here's what happens next:
> 1. **Your dashboard is live** — [Login Link]
> 2. **Kickoff call** — We'll reach out within 24 hours to schedule your 30-minute setup call
> 3. **Quick win** — Check out [specific feature] to [specific benefit] today
>
> You've got a dedicated success rep: [Rep Name] — reply to this email anytime.
>
> Let's go.
> — The Dream AI Team

## Days 2–3: Kickoff & Goal Alignment

### Kickoff Call Agenda (30 min)
1. **Introductions** — who's who (2 min)
2. **Goals** — what does success look like for them in 30/60/90 days? (5 min)
3. **Current state** — what are they doing now? What's painful? (5 min)
4. **Dream AI setup walkthrough** — live dashboard tour (10 min)
5. **First action together** — do one thing live on the call (5 min)
6. **Next steps & schedule** — set Week 1 milestones (3 min)

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Record goals in CRM (fields: primary_goal, secondary_goal, success_metric)
- [ ] Create client success plan doc
- [ ] Tag client segment: beginner / intermediate / power-user
- [ ] Schedule Day 7 check-in

### Email — Post-Kickoff

> **Subject:** Your kickoff notes + next step
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> Great call today. Here's what we covered:
> - Your goal: [specific goal]
> - First milestone: [specific action] by [date]
> - Key feature to focus on: [feature]
>
> Your one assignment this week: [specific action]
>
> Ping me if you hit any walls.
> — [Rep Name]

---

## Days 4–7: First Value Milestone

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Monitor dashboard for login activity (flag if no login by Day 3)
- [ ] Send Day 4 check-in message (async, not a call)
- [ ] Verify client has completed first key action
- [ ] If no activity → trigger "stuck" sequence (see below)

### Day 4 Check-in Email

> **Subject:** Quick check — how's it going?
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> Just checking in. Have you had a chance to [first action]?
>
> If you're stuck, reply and I'll walk you through it. No question too small.
>
> — [Rep Name]

### "Stuck" Sequence (if no activity by Day 5)
1. **Day 5:** Personal message (email or SMS) — "Noticed you haven't logged in yet. Everything okay?"
2. **Day 6:** Send a 2-min Loom/walkthrough video for the first action
3. **Day 7:** Phone call if still no activity

### Success Criteria — Week 1
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Dashboard logins | ≥ 3 |
| First key action completed | Yes |
| Kickoff call attended | Yes |

## Days 8–14: Build the Habit

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Day 7 check-in call completed
- [ ] Confirm client is using core features (not just logging in)
- [ ] Share one case study from their niche
- [ ] Identify any feature gaps or requests
- [ ] Schedule Day 14 review call

### Day 7 Check-in Call Agenda (15 min)
1. Quick wins so far — what's working? (5 min)
2. Friction points — anything annoying or confusing? (5 min)
3. Assign Week 2 action — deeper feature usage (3 min)
4. Confirm Day 14 review schedule (2 min)

### Email — Week 2 Value Proof

> **Subject:** Here's proof this is working (even this early)
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> Quick update from your first week:
> - [Metric 1]: [result]
> - [Metric 2]: [result]
>
> [Similar client in your niche] saw [specific result] in their first two weeks. You're on the same track.
>
> Your next step: [specific action for Week 2]
>
> — [Rep Name]

### Success Criteria — Week 2
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Core feature usage | ≥ 3 different features used |
| Result logged (lead, sale, time saved, etc.) | At least 1 positive data point |
| Engagement score | ≥ 50 (see Health Scoring doc) |

## Days 15–21: Deepen Engagement

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Day 14 review call completed
- [ ] Document wins in CRM (for testimonials/case studies later)
- [ ] Introduce one advanced feature or automation
- [ ] Add to "active clients" Slack channel (if applicable)
- [ ] Flag any health score concerns

### Day 14 Review Call Agenda (30 min)
1. **Results recap** — what's happened since kickoff? (10 min)
2. **ROI check** — are they seeing value relative to cost? (5 min)
3. **Advanced setup** — introduce 1 power feature relevant to their goals (10 min)
4. **Referral check** — "Who else should know about this?" (5 min)

### Email — Post Review

> **Subject:** Your 2-week results + what's next
>
> Hey [First Name],
>
> Two weeks in. Here's what you've accomplished:
> - [Win 1]
> - [Win 2]
> - [Win 3]
>
> Here's what I want you focused on for the next two weeks:
> - [Advanced action 1]
> - [Advanced action 2]
>
> You're ahead of schedule. Let's keep the momentum.
>
> — [Rep Name]

### Success Criteria — Week 3
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Feature adoption depth | Using ≥ 5 core features |
| ROI conversation | Client can articulate value |
| Health score | ≥ 65 |

## Days 22–30: Lock In Retention

### Internal Checklist
- [ ] Day 21 async check-in sent
- [ ] Prepare 30-day success report
- [ ] Identify expansion opportunity (higher tier, add-ons)
- [ ] Send NPS survey (Day 28)
- [ ] Schedule 30-day review / quarterly planning call
- [ ] Request testimonial or case study if results are strong

### 30-Day Review Call Agenda (30 min)
1. **Celebrate wins** — review the success report together (10 min)
2. **Gap analysis** — what didn't get done? Why? (5 min)
3. **Q2 roadmap** — what's next for them? (10 min)
4. **Expansion** — introduce higher tier or add-on if appropriate (5 min)

### Success Criteria — Day 30 (Onboarding Complete)
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Primary goal progress | ≥ 1 major milestone achieved |
| Health score | ≥ 70 |
| Feature adoption | ≥ 60% of core features |
| NPS | ≥ 8 (promoter) |
| Auto-renew confidence | High |

## Onboarding KPIs (Internal)

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Kickoff call completion rate | ≥ 95% | % of new clients who attend |
| Day 14 activation rate | ≥ 80% | % with ≥ 1 positive result logged |
| Day 30 health score ≥ 70 | ≥ 75% | Health scoring system |
| 30-day churn | < 5% | % who cancel within 30 days |
| Time to first value | < 48 hours | Hours from purchase to first result |

---

## Escalation Rules

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| No login by Day 3 | Escalate to success manager + founder |
| Kickoff call no-show | Reschedule once, then founder outreach |
| Health score < 40 at Day 14 | Emergency check-in call |
| Negative NPS (< 6) | Founder call within 24 hours |
| Request for refund in first 30 days | Pause, escalate to founder for retention call |
