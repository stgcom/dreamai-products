Setup Guide

Part 1: Twilio Configuration

Twilio handles all SMS sending and receiving. Set it up once, and the automation runs forever.

1

Create a Twilio Account

Sign up at twilio.com and verify your account. You'll get $15 in free trial credit to test with.

2

Buy a Phone Number

Navigate to Phone Numbers → Buy a Number. Choose a local number with SMS capabilities. This will be your business line.

3

Set Up a Messaging Service

Go to Messaging Services → Create. Add your purchased number. This groups your number and webhook into one unit.

4

Configure the Webhook

In your Messaging Service settings, set the Inbound webhook URL to your N8N webhook endpoint (provided in the workflow JSON below). Set it to POST.

5

Grab Your Credentials

From the Twilio Console dashboard, copy your Account SID, Auth Token, and the Messaging Service SID. You'll paste these into the N8N workflow.

Part 2: Google Calendar Integration

The workflow checks real-time availability and creates bookings directly in your Google Calendar.

1

Create a Dedicated Calendar

In Google Calendar, create a new calendar called "SMS Bookings". This keeps automated bookings separate from your personal events.

2

Set Up Google Cloud Credentials

Go to console.cloud.google.com. Create a project (or use an existing one), enable the Google Calendar API, and create an OAuth 2.0 credential (Desktop app type).

3

Connect to N8N

In N8N, go to Credentials → Add → Google Calendar OAuth2. Enter your Client ID and Secret from Google Cloud. Authorize access and select your "SMS Bookings" calendar.

4

Set Business Hours

Configure your available hours in the N8N workflow settings. Default is Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM. Adjust the time slots and buffer duration between appointments.

5

Test the Integration

Send a test SMS to your Twilio number. The workflow should check the calendar, find an open slot, and create a test event. Verify it appears in your Google Calendar.

N8N Workflow JSON

Copy this workflow and import it directly into your N8N instance. It contains the complete automation: receive SMS → parse intent → check calendar → book → confirm → remind → follow-up → request review.

Configuration

Environment Variables

Set these in your N8N instance settings (Settings → Environment Variables) before activating the workflow.

🔑

Required Variables

  • TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID — Your Twilio Account SID (starts with AC)
  • TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN — Your Twilio Auth Token
  • TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER — Your Twilio phone number (e.g., +15551234567)
  • TWILIO_MESSAGING_SERVICE_SID — Messaging Service SID (starts with MG)
⚙️

Business Configuration

  • BUSINESS_NAME — Your company name
  • BUSINESS_ADDRESS — Your physical address
  • REVIEW_LINK — Direct link to your review page
  • BOOKING_WINDOW_HOURS — Hours before appointment to stop booking (default: 2)
  • REMINDER_HOURS_BEFORE — Hours before appointment to send reminder (default: 24)
