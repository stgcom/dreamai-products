4. Google Review Link Setup

1

Find Your Place ID

Go to Google Maps → search your business → URL contains your Place ID

2

Create Short Link

Use bit.ly or your own domain. Format: yourplace.com/review

3

Generate QR Code

Use a free QR generator. Print on receipts, cards, signage

4

Add Everywhere

Email signature, business card, receipt, follow-up text, in-store sign

Pro tip: The shorter your link, the higher the click rate. Use a custom domain if possible (e.g., review.yourbusiness.com).

5. N8N Automation Workflow

Connect your CRM/service system to automate review requests:

①

Trigger

Service completed → webhook fires

②

Wait

24-hour delay (give customer time to experience results)

③

Send SMS

Personalized review request via Twilio

④

Wait 48h

Check if review was left (via Google Places API)

⑤

Follow Up

If no review → send email follow-up

⑥

Track

Log to spreadsheet: sent, opened, review left (Y/N), rating

{
  "name": "Review Request Automation",
  "nodes": [
    {"type": "webhook", "name": "Service Complete", "parameters": {"path": "/service-complete"}},
    {"type": "wait", "name": "Wait 24 Hours", "parameters": {"hours": 24}},
    {"type": "twilio", "name": "Send Review SMS", "parameters": {"to": "{{$json.customer_phone}}", "body": "Hi {{$json.customer_name}}! Thanks for choosing us. If you're happy with your service, a quick Google review means the world: {{$json.review_link}}"}},
    {"type": "wait", "name": "Wait 48 Hours", "parameters": {"hours": 48}},
    {"type": "if", "name": "Check Review Status", "parameters": {"condition": "review_left == false"}},
    {"type": "email", "name": "Follow-up Email", "parameters": {"to": "{{$json.customer_email}}", "subject": "Quick favor?", "body": "Hi {{$json.customer_name}}, just following up — would you mind leaving us a Google review? It really helps! {{$json.review_link}}"}}
  ]
