Follow-Up Scripts

Booking, reminders, reschedules & after-hours capture

Lesson 2 of 540%

📖 In This Lesson

You'll master 7 scripts for everything that happens after the initial greeting — booking appointments, handling reschedules, sending reminders, capturing after-hours emergencies, and following up the next day.

📅
Booking 4 Scripts
11
Appointment Confirmation
When a new appointment is being booked
Perfect, [FirstName]! I have you down for [DayOfWeek], [Date] at [Time] for a [ServiceType]. You'll receive a confirmation text at this number. We ask that you arrive about 5 minutes early. Is there anything else I can help with?
Customize: Add location or parking instructions if relevant. For telehealth: "You'll receive a video link via email 15 minutes before your appointment."
12
Reschedule Request
When a caller needs to move an existing appointment
Absolutely, I can help with that. I see your current appointment is on [CurrentDate] at [CurrentTime]. Would you like to move it to a specific date and time, or would you prefer I show you the next available openings?
Customize: Set a cancellation policy reminder: "Just a note — we do have a [hours]-hour cancellation window."
13
Appointment Reminder
Automated call/text before an appointment
Hi [FirstName], this is [AIName] from [BusinessName]. Just a friendly reminder: your [ServiceType] appointment is tomorrow, [Date], at [Time]. Reply 1 to confirm or 2 to reschedule. We look forward to seeing you!
Customize: Send 24–48 hours before. If no response, trigger a second reminder 3 hours before. Include any prep instructions: fasting, documents to bring, arrival time.
14
Follow-Up Call
After an appointment or service has been completed
Hi [FirstName], this is [AIName] from [BusinessName]. I'm following up on your visit on [VisitDate]. How did everything go? Is there anything you need from us — a follow-up appointment, additional information, or any concerns we should know about?
Customize: Best sent 24–72 hours post-visit. If positive experience, transition to a review request. If concerns, escalate to a human immediately.

After-Hours 3 Scripts
15
After-Hours Auto-Response
Default message when the business is closed
Thank you for calling [BusinessName]. Our office is currently closed. We're open [Hours]. Your call is important to us — please stay on the line to leave your name, number, and a brief message, and we'll return your call first thing when we open. If this is an emergency, press 1 to be connected to our on-call team.
Customize: Fill in [Hours] with actual business hours (e.g., "Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 6 PM"). The press-1 option routes to an on-call human or priority intake form.
16
Emergency Capture
Collecting urgent caller information after hours
I understand this is urgent. Let me make sure we capture everything so our team can respond as quickly as possible. Please state your full name, the nature of the emergency, and the best callback number. I'm recording this now.
Customize: After capturing, immediately text/pager the on-call staff. Add location/address capture if you serve on-site. Tag as HIGH PRIORITY in your CRM.
17
Next-Day Callback Confirmation
After a voicemail or after-hours message is left
Hi [FirstName], this is [AIName] from [BusinessName]. Thank you for your call yesterday. I'm reaching out to follow up on your message about [Topic]. How can I help you today?
Customize: Send within 30 minutes of opening. Reference the specific topic from their message to show attentiveness. If no answer, leave as voicemail and send follow-up text.
