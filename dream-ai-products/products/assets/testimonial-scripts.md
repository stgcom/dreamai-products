## Production Notes

- Record on smartphone (vertical for Reels/TikTok, horizontal for YouTube/landing page)
- Natural lighting, clean background
- Real-sounding, not scripted-feeling (encourage ad-libbing)
- Add captions/subtitles (85% of social video watched without sound)
- Crop to 9:16 for social, 16:9 for landing page embed
- Dream AI intro/outro branding (1 sec each)
