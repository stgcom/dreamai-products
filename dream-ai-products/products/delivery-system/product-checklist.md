## Launch Sequence

When all items are checked, follow this order:

1. **Go live in Stripe** — Switch from test to live keys
2. **Update webhook** — Point to production N8N with live signing secret
3. **Update PostgreSQL** — Replace test `stripe_product_id` with live IDs
4. **Activate N8N workflow** — Set to active in production
5. **Soft launch** — Share with 5-10 people, monitor for issues
6. **Public launch** — Enable on website, share broadly
7. **Monitor** — Check N8N executions, sales_log, delivery_errors for 48h
